  1  """ 
  2  The ``zen.io.gml`` module (available as ``zen.gml``) supports the reading and  
  3  writing network data in the `Graph Modeling Language (GML)  
  4  <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graph_Modelling_Language>`_.  At present only  
  5  reading GML is supported. 
  6
