 1  """ 
 2  The ``zen.control`` package provides functions and classes for analyzing the control structure of a network.  In particular, much of the 
 3  functionality currently implemented focuses on the perspective and machinery provided by structural controllability. 
 4   
 5  *This package is currently under active development and will be regularly updated, though the API specified will remain stable.* 
 6   
 7  Reachability 
 8  ------------ 
 9   
10  Under structural controllability, a set of directly controlled nodes can control a limited set of other nodes.  The  
11  number of such nodes that can be controlled is called the *reachability* or *generic rank* of the network under those 
12  controls. 
13   
14  .. autofunction:: num_min_controls(G) 
15   
16  Control Profiles 
17  ---------------- 
18   
19  Control profiles were devised as a measure for quantifying the structures responsible for dictating how many 
20  controls a network requires and where these controls much attach to the network. 
21   
22  .. seealso:: 
23   
24          J. Ruths and D. Ruths (2014). Control Profiles of Complex Networks. Science, 343(6177), 1373-1376. 
25   
26  .. autofunction:: profile(G,...) 
27   
28  Visualizing control profile plots can be particularly helpful when comparing the control profiles of different 
29  networks.  Two functions are provided for this purpose. 
30   
31  .. autofunction:: profile_plot(G,...) 
32   
33  .. autofunction:: profile_heatmap(G,...)
